F.A.Q

Quickly find the answers you seek.

Why are you so expensive?

The process we use to do your project takes a certain amount of time and a lot of planning and research. Unlike other agencies, we don't use templates we design your projects from the scratch, which means you get a unique design tailored around your business.

Why are you so cheap?

Yep. We have also been asked that question too. We're typically cheaper than larger agencies because...

1. We work remotely which means you don't pay for water cooler or beanbag chairs.
2. We're a small team who take on a limited amount of clients each year. You won't be talking to account managers, rather you'll be talking to the team directly.

Do you offer payment schedule?

Yes, we split the payment into two. The first 50% is usually taken once you have seen the mockup of your project and you're happy to move forward. The following 50% is taken 30 days after this.

Can i make final payment when project is ready?

Sorry but that's a No. In the past we have found out that projects can take longer than expected to complete. We might be waiting for information from you and this can delay the process. Sometimes these delays can take months, this is why we always invoice 30days after the original payment. At this point we'll be well on our way with your new project and you'll be able to see the progress.

What are your modes of payment?

Currently we don't have a payment gateway intergrated in our website, we are currently working on that, for now our payments are received directly into our bank account, through direct bank transfers or bank deposits. Let us know if you have more questions by contacting us through our support line. support@techzoid.com

Do i sorely own my project after payments?

Yes. Upon receiving your final payment, the copyrigyht and ownership of your project is transfered to you.
