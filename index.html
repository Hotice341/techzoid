F.A.Q

Quickly find the answers you seek.

What is your web design duration?

It takes approximately 6-10 weeks to build a website from start to finish, providing we have all the imformation from you. Generally speaking the website will only take along time if we are still waiting for text and images from you.

How do we talk during my website build process?

Before we begin working together, we usually have an initial chat on the phone or via Skype to discuss the project. Once we get started, most of the communication will take place over email.

What will you need from me?

It really depends on what type of website you want. We'll be able to discuss this on our discovery call before we start working together. Depending on which package we agree on, we could need...


1. Images of you and your team
2. Images of your workplace
3. Content for your website
4. Staff profiles
5. Any videos you would like added to the website etc.

You can see more on our project-request page.
Who hosts my website?

If we build your website, we will generally manage the hosting for you so you don't need to do anything. We use the same hosting company for our own website and for all our clients. Each website is managed individually to avoid any security issues.

Can you manage my site for me?

Yes we can arrange a maintenance package to suit your needs. This can range from 1hr per month to 10hrs per month and we can discuss a package that's right for you.

Will you meet me face to face?

We are always happy to meet you in person if you really need us to meet, we'll arrange a consultation price for travel and possible accomodation depending on the distance.
